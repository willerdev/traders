# Video generator prompt — Sell your setup, earn on full TP

**Use this prompt as-is (or lightly edit the brand name) with Runway, Pika, Synthesia, HeyGen, CapCut AI, etc.**

---

## Prompt

**Length:** 75–90 seconds · **Format:** 9:16 vertical + 16:9 cut · **Tone:** fast, confident, trader-focused · **Music:** upbeat electronic · **Style:** clean fintech UI mockups, chart overlays, mobile app screens

**Title card (3s):** “Submit your setup. Hit TP. Get paid.”

**Voiceover + visuals — scene by scene:**

1. **Hook (5s)** — Text: “You don’t need a broker deal to monetize your edge.” Show a trader marking entry, stop, and take profit on a chart. VO: “Post your trade idea before it runs — and earn when you’re right.”

2. **Sign up & funded account (8s)** — Quick register screen. VO: “Join with a small one-time fee. You get a virtual funded account — start at $1,000, fixed 5% risk per trade, scale up to $25,000 as you rank.” Show Bronze → Elite tier ladder and leaderboard flash.

3. **Submit your setup (12s)** — Submit Signal flow: symbol, BUY/SELL, entry zone, stop loss, take profit, chart screenshot, short analysis. VO: “Sell your setup — submit entry, SL, and TP before execution. Every signal is timestamped and locked. No edits, no cheating.” Highlight **Risk/Reward from 1:1 up to 1:5+** on the form. On-screen badges: “1:2 +5 pts · 1:3 +10 pts · 1:4+ +15 pts” (leaderboard bonus).

4. **Platform runs it on MT5 (10s)** — MT5 tab: Setups → **Place on MT5** (limit or running). VO: “We queue your idea on platform MT5 — limits while price waits, live trades when it fills.” Quick cuts: running P/L refreshing, breakeven, partial close, full close.

5. **Win paths & money (18s)** — Split screen:
   - **Full TP:** price hits your full take profit → TP Claims → upload before/after screenshots → approval → **cash TP reward** (USDT) → request payout.
   - **1:1 RR path:** price hits TP1 (1:1) → optional breakeven → claim **1:1 RR** (smaller reward, still counts as a win) → keep climbing stats.
   VO: “Hit **full TP** for the full reward. Reach **1:1 RR** first? Claim that too — secure breakeven, bank partial credit, stay in the game. Higher RR setups (up to **1:5**) earn bigger leaderboard points when they win.”

6. **Weekly payout tiers (10s)** — Payout reward card: last 10 setups → **$10 / $50 / $100 USDT** tiers (7 wins = Pro, 8+ = Elite). VO: “Stack wins. Weekly USDT tiers reward consistency — not just one lucky trade.”

7. **Trust & competition (8s)** — Icons: duplicate detection, screenshot proof, fair rankings. VO: “Anti-cheat, verified claims, public leaderboard — prove your edge, get seen.”

8. **CTA (6s)** — Logo + URL. VO: “Submit your next setup today. Prove it. Get funded. Get paid.” Button text: **“Start trading — Register now”**

**On-screen text to include (brief flashes):** Submit setup · 1:1–1:5 RR · MT5 live · Full TP reward · 1:1 RR claim · Breakeven · Partial close · TP Claims · Weekly USDT · Leaderboard · No broker required

**Do not show:** guaranteed income, “risk-free,” or specific dollar promises beyond “earn on approved full TP / tiered weekly rewards.”

## One-paragraph version (if the tool has a character limit)

Create a 80-second vertical promo for a trader talent platform. A trader submits a locked setup (entry zone, SL, TP, chart screenshot) with risk/reward from 1:1 to 1:5+ before execution. They get a virtual funded account (5% risk, rank from Bronze to Elite). The platform executes on MT5 (limits and running trades; breakeven, partial and full close). Earnings: full take-profit claims with screenshot proof and USDT reward; optional 1:1 RR claim at TP1 with breakeven; RR bonuses on wins (1:2/+5 pts, 1:3/+10, 1:4+/+15); weekly USDT payout tiers ($10/$50/$100 based on wins in last 10 setups). Show leaderboard, anti-cheat, and mobile-friendly MT5 + Submit + TP Claims UI. Tone: professional, energetic. End CTA: register and submit your first setup.
